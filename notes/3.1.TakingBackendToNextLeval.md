## 2. The Mega Project: "YouTube Clone" Backend
The final project will be a fully functional video hosting platform (similar to YouTube) combined with social media features (similar to Twitter/X).

### **Project Scope**
*   **Scale:** The frontend design (UI) contains **29+ Screens**, implying a highly complex backend to support these views.
*   **Core Functionality:** A mix of Video streaming, Social networking, and Content management.

### **Key Modules & Features**

#### **A. Authentication & User Management (The Foundation)**
*   *Estimate:* Hitesh estimates **25-30%** of the backend work lies here.
*   **Features:**
    *   Registration & Login.
    *   **Security:** Access Tokens, Refresh Tokens, Bearer Tokens.
    *   **Session Management:** Cookies and password protection mechanisms.
    *   Email handling.

#### **B. Video Management**
*   **Uploads:** Drag and drop functionality for video files.
*   **Metadata:** Handling Thumbnails, Titles, Descriptions.
*   **Visibility:** Public/Private publishing states.
*   **Storage:** Handling heavy files (likely using Cloudinary/AWS S3, though not explicitly named in this specific video, it is implied by "production grade").

#### **C. User Interaction (Social Features)**
*   **History:** Tracking watch history.
*   **Playlists:** Users can create multiple playlists and add videos to them.
*   **Subscriptions:** Managing subscriber counts and channel relationships.
*   **Likes/Dislikes:** Toggling states on videos.
*   **Comments:** Commenting on videos (and potentially nested comments).

#### **D. The "Tweet" Feature**
*   **Purpose:** To cover text-based social media logic (like Twitter) within the same app.
*   **Functionality:** Community posts/Tweets that can also receive comments and likes.

#### **E. Admin Dashboard**
*   **Analytics:** A dashboard for the channel owner.
*   **Stats:** Total views, total subscribers, total likes across all videos.
*   **Video Management:** Edit, Delete, or update status of uploaded videos.

***

## 3. Key Technical Concepts to be Covered
To build this, the series will dive deep into specific MongoDB and Mongoose features:

1.  **Aggregation Pipelines:**
    *   This is a major focus for the Dashboard.
    *   *Use Case:* calculating complex stats (e.g., "Sum of all views from all videos where `owner_id` matches X").
2.  **Data Modeling:**
    *   Structuring complex relationships (Users <-> Videos <-> Playlists <-> Comments).
3.  **File Handling:**
    *   Professional handling of images and video files.
